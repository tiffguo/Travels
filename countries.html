Here's where I've been...


LONDON

London

Check out Tower Bridge, Westminster Abbey, Buckingham Palace, and the famed Big Ben. Go luxury shopping at Harrods and Selfridges, and don't forget to "mind the gap" on the tube!

SANTORINI

Santorini

Santorini is the original Atlantis is one of the most popular honeymoon destinations for young lovers. Hike from Oia to Fira, and indulge in Santorini's ultimate gastronomic experiences!

AMALFI

Amalfi

Amalfi is a town in a dramatic natural setting below steep cliffs on Italy’s southwest coast. Looking around the colorful town, lemons show up in regional ceramics and a citrus scent fills the air as you meander about.

COPENHAGEN

Copenhagen

This is where old fairy tales blend with flashy new architecture and world-class design; where warm jazz mixes with cold electronica from Copenhagen's basements. Explore hipster Nyhavn and Tivoli Gardens!

LISBON

Lisbon

This beautiful city is blessed with buzzing nightlife (check out Pink Street!), take a tile painting class, eat a pastel de nata, and check out the world's second biggest aquarium.

PAROS

Paros

Paros is a Greek island in the Aegean Sea best known for its beaches and traditional villages. Be sure to browse the mythology imbued evil eye jewelry and take in the white-and-blue colored landscape!

FLORENCE

Florence

Florence is home to masterpieces of Renaissance art and architecture, like Michelangelo’s “David” sculpture, “The Birth of Venus”, and Vinci’s “Annunciation" in all of its galleries and the Duomo.

GUANGZHOU

GuangZhou

My parents are from Guangzhou, so this is a city that is also dear to me! This buzzing, vibrant city features avant-garde architecture and (in my opinion) the best food in the world.

BATH

Bath

Bath is the largest city in the county of Somerset, England, known for and named after its Roman-built baths. Bathe in the modern Thermae Bath Spa, the only natural thermal hot srpings in Britain.

TORONTO

Toronto

Toronto, my birthplace, holds a dear place in my heart. I spent my early childhood wandering around Pacific Mall, full of Asian goods. Observe the bustling streets and the famous waterfront skyline dominated by the CN Tower.

CANCUN

Cancun

Cancún is a vacation resort town, an adventure base for exploring lagoons and caves and boasts open-air museum showcasing the ruined temples of the Mayans. I made many great memories with my friends here for spring break.

BEIJING

Beijing

Beijing, China’s sprawling capital, has history stretching back 3 millennia. Check out ancient sites such as the grand Forbidden City complex and the Imperial Palace during the Ming and Qing dynasties.
