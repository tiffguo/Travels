Countries and their History

Here is the amazing history of the countries that I've explored!

United Kingdom

The United Kingdom is an island nation located in the Atlantic Ocean just off the coast of France. London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom and one of the largest and most important cities in the world.

Italy

Italy’s long history is dominated by three periods, the three R’s: Roman Empire, Renaissance, and Risorgimento. Florence was founded as a Roman military colony about the 1st century bce, and during its long history it has been a republic, a seat of the duchy of Tuscany, and a capital (1865–70) of Italy. During the 14th–16th century Florence achieved preeminence in commerce and finance, learning, and especially the arts.

Greece

Greece is one of the oldest civilizations in the world and the cradle of Western culture as we know it. The first signs of inhabitance were the Cycladic, Minoan and Mycenaean maritime civilisations that lived and ruled during the Bronze Age (3000-1200 BC).
